About Us

Assets Recovery Kenya is a procedural asset and financial assistance service helping individuals, families and estates lawfully identify and reclaim unclaimed financial assets in Kenya, working as agents. Frequently Asked Questions

What are unclaimed surplus funds?

Unclaimed surplus funds are money or assets (like bank deposits, insurance payouts, or investment dividends) that rightfully belong to an individual or entity but haven't been claimed after a set period, often due to lost contact, forgotten accounts, or death, eventually being turned over to government agencies for safekeeping until the rightful owner claims them. Specific "surplus funds" can also refer to money left over after a property is sold in a foreclosure, exceeding the debt owed. .

How can I recover my money or assets?

Reach out to us and we shall get in touch and initiate the process of recovering your money/assets. We do all the work and follow up so that you don't have to.

How long does recovery take?

Timelines vary depending on asset type and documentation. Some take days, others weeks, while complex estates may take longer under verification, but we certainly do deliver.

How much does recovery cost?

We operate on a contingency-based fee arrangement agreed upon with the client and put in writing as a contract upon client's request before starting the process.
